The Story Structure

THE MATCH
Setup
You meet your Mortal and their potential Match. I'll show you their charts, histories, contradictions. Then the Compatibility Matrix—what could work, what could crash, what your job is. This is where you learn the odds. The stars have opinions. So do I.
SCENE 1: The Meet-Cute
First encounter
Where and how they first meet. First impressions. Initial attraction—or initial disaster.
SCENE 2: The Date Begins
Chemistry unfolds
Arrival. Greeting. Initial chemistry. Small talk that needs to become something more. Romantic attraction meets physical chemistry.
SCENE 3: The Turning Point
Vulnerability
Real conversation. The moment where connection either crystallizes or dissolves. Can they handle each other's truth?
THE DATE ENDS & CUPID'S EVALUATION
Final reckoning
Goodbye choreography. The walk to the subway. The moment face to face. Does someone go in for the kiss? Physical chemistry meets accumulated trust. I calculate the final score, predict their future, and start building your astrological profile.
