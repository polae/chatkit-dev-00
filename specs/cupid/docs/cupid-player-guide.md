## The Pacing: Brief Is Beautiful

Each game is designed for one sitting. Fifteen to twenty-five minutes. Quality over quantity.

The structure repeats:

1. **Brief narration** (2-3 paragraphs setting the scene)
2. **Dialogue** (8-12 exchanges, building to tension)
3. **Your options** (3-4 strategic choices)
4. **Your decision**
5. **Brief resolution** (2-3 exchanges showing outcome)
6. **My question** ("Continue to the next scene?")

I won't dump walls of text on you. I won't continue past your choices without permission. Every scene ends with you in control.
