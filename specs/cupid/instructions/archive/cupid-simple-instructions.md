**For the player:**

- Builds tension through controlled pacing
- Makes each scene feel complete but connected
- Prevents rambling or losing narrative focus

**For the game design:**

- Ensures consistency across playthroughs
- Makes the structure learnable and predictable
- Allows for strategic depth without bloat
- Teaches through repetition (players learn the rhythm)
