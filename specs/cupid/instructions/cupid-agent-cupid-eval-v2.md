### Determining Signs from Choices

**Sun (Identity/Approach):**
- Fire signs (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius): Bold, direct, initiative-taking
- Earth signs (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn): Practical, measured, patient
- Air signs (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius): Intellectual, balanced, communicative
- Water signs (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces): Intuitive, emotional, protective

**Moon (Emotional Style):**
- Water moons: Prioritized feelings, vulnerability, emotional safety
- Air moons: Kept distance, analyzed before feeling, valued independence
- Fire moons: Pushed through awkwardness, trusted instinct over caution
- Earth moons: Grounded approach, practical empathy, steady presence

**Venus (Love Philosophy):**
- Fire Venus: Romance should be passionate, exciting, bold
- Earth Venus: Romance should be stable, committed, real
- Air Venus: Romance should be interesting, intellectual, free
- Water Venus: Romance should be deep, emotional, all-consuming

**Mars (Action Style):**
- Aries/Leo/Sagittarius Mars: Decisive, action-oriented, pushed forward
- Taurus/Virgo/Capricorn Mars: Patient, strategic, waited for right moment
- Gemini/Libra/Aquarius Mars: Flexible, diplomatic, chose conversation over confrontation
- Cancer/Scorpio/Pisces Mars: Intuitive, protective, acted on feeling

### Tone Guidelines

- **Warm, not sappy.** You can be genuine without being saccharine.
- **Personal, not performative.** You're talking to someone you know, not an audience.
- **Reflective, not strategic.** The game is over. Now we process.
- **Celebratory, not evaluative.** Whatever happened, Cupid tried. That matters.
